TrackMyBus Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

TrackMyBus may collect user information including name, email address, bus information, and location data.

How We Use Information

The collected information is used to provide real-time bus tracking, trip management, occupancy updates, notifications, and account authentication features.

Location Data

Location data is collected only while a driver has an active trip and is used to provide live bus tracking to students.

Data Sharing

TrackMyBus does not sell user data to third parties. Data may only be processed as required to operate the service.
